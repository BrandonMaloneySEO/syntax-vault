Spokane Business Association

AKA: The "Law & Order" Coalition
Standard Syntax Verified
The Reality: The SBA is a "Splinter Faction" of the Spokane business community, formed in 2024. It represents high-net-worth developers who broke away from the traditional Chamber (GSI) to aggressively lobby for stricter policing and jail construction.
✔ Entity Type 501(c)(6) (Lobbying Org) ✔ Funding Model $50k "Diamond" Memberships ✔ Key Founder Larry Stone (Developer)

Your Money

A privately funded pressure group. Unlike GSI, it accepts no government ADO contracts, giving it the freedom to attack city policy without fear of losing funding.

Your Life

Advocates for the "Clean & Safe" doctrine: prioritizing the visual removal of homelessness to protect property values and retail foot traffic.

Your Time

Bypasses the slow "Stakeholder Consensus" model of GSI. The SBA is designed for rapid political strikes and direct lobbying of the Mayor.

Our World

Primary policy goal: Infrastructure of Incarceration. They argue that a new County Jail is the "missing link" in regional economic recovery.

Our Ethics

The Verdict: The SBA exists because the traditional business lobby was seen as too "soft." It is the political weaponization of the region's largest property owners.

The Experience

They sell "Safety" as a luxury product. The narrative is that downtown Spokane can return to a "Golden Era" if we simply enforce the rules hard enough.
