Stichting INGKA Foundation

Trading as: IKEA
Standard Syntax Verified
The Reality: IKEA is wholly owned by a Dutch Non-Profit Foundation. This complex legal structure is designed to secure the company's longevity, preventing it from being sold or taken public, while optimizing tax obligations under Dutch law.
✔ Legal Entity Stichting (Foundation) ✔ Resource Scale 1% Global Wood Supply ✔ Jurisdiction Leiden, Netherlands

Your Money

Profits are retained within the Foundation to ensure independence. The structure minimizes exposure to global corporate tax rates.

Your Life

The "Democratic Design" philosophy prioritizes low price over longevity, creating a cycle of consumption.

Your Time

Retail layouts ("The Fixed Path") are scientifically designed to maximize dwell time and impulse purchases.

Our World

IKEA consumes approx. 15 million m³ of wood annually (1% of global supply).

Our Ethics

The Verdict: While legally compliant, the use of a charitable vehicle for corporate defense creates a significant opacity in financial reporting compared to public companies.

The Experience

A controlled environment where the "Hot Dog Index" (pricing food below cost) is used to anchor the perception of value for furniture.
