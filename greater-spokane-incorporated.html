Greater Spokane Incorporated

Classification: 501(c)(6) Trade Association
Standard Syntax Verified
The Reality: GSI is not a government agency. It is a Private Trade Association formed by the merger of the Chamber of Commerce and the Economic Development Council. It receives public tax funding to execute private sector recruitment.
✔ Tax Status 501(c)(6) Non-Profit ✔ Structure Private Membership Org ✔ Public Role Associate Dev. Org (ADO)

Your Money

Funded by a hybrid of private dues (buying influence) and public contracts. Taxpayers effectively subsidize the lobbying efforts of the region's largest corporations.

Your Life

GSI dictates the region's "Growth Strategy," often prioritizing industrial land expansion and big-box recruitment over neighborhood-level economic resilience.

Your Time

The committee structure acts as a "Time Sink" for dissenters. Real decisions are often pre-determined by the Executive Committee (Top-Tier Investors).

Our World

A staunch advocate for the North South Freeway (NSF), prioritizing freight corridors for logistics companies over the urban fabric of East Central Spokane.

Our Ethics

The Verdict: By merging the Chamber (Private Interest) and the EDC (Public Interest), GSI created a structural conflict of interest. They are paid by the public to serve the private.

The Experience

The "Annual Meeting" functions as a theater of consensus, designed to present a unified regional narrative that masks the diverging interests of labor and capital.
