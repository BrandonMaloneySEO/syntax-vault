The Overton Window

Cognition ID: COG-OVW
Standard Syntax Verified
The Definition: A model for understanding how ideas transform from "Unthinkable" to "Policy." It is the narrow range of ideas that are politically acceptable at any given time. Politicians rarely lead the window; they merely stand inside it to survive.

Your Money

Fiscal policy is often a lagging indicator of the window. Trillion-dollar deficits were once political suicide; the window shifted, and now they are standard procedure.

Your Life

The window dictates your social safety. Expressing an opinion outside the window (even if factually true) leads to social ostracization. You police your own speech to stay inside.

The Mechanism

"The Door-in-the-Face": Radicals propose something insane (The Door) so that the slightly less insane thing (The Compromise) seems reasonable.

Our World

The Shift: Look at the speed of legalization (Weed, Sports Gambling). These ideas spent 50 years as "Criminal" and 5 years shifting to "Taxable Revenue."

The Antidote (Human Application)

Frame Awareness: Recognize that "Normal" is not a moral truth; it is a temporary coordinate. Do not confuse "Socially Acceptable" with "Factually Correct."
